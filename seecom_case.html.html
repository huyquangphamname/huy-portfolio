Fragmented Data & Operational Blindspots

Operational decision-making was hindered by siloed data sources. Critical business queries regarding inventory levels and fulfillment rates faced a 48-hour latency due to manual compilation from disparate systems.

  • • High risk of spoilage due to delayed reporting.
  • • Excessive manual hours spent on Excel maintenance.
  • • Lack of "Single Source of Truth" leading to conflicting KPIs.
